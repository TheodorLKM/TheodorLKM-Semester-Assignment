Schools & Groups

Bring science to life with curriculum-linked workshops for key stages 2-3. Our team will help tailor your visit to your learners.

Workshop menu

  • Forces & Motion (KS2)
    Build mini-roller coasters and test variables like height and weight.
  • Lights & Space (KS2-KS3)
    Model phases of the moon and explore constellations in our mini-dome.
  • Micro World
    Use a microscope to observe cells, draw and label observations.
  • Maker Challenge (KS2-KS3)
    Design a simple circuit to solve a real-world problem.

Facilities for groups

  • Coach drop-off point and packed-lunch space.
  • Lockers and cloakroom
  • Step-free access throughout.

Risk assessment notes

  • All workshops are supervised by trained professionals.
  • First aiders on sight, evacuation points signed.
  • Maximum group size per workshop (30).

How to book

  1. 1-2 preferred dates (Wed-Fri term-time).
  2. Pick a workshop and note your year group and numbers.
  3. Send details via our contact formand we'll confirm within two business days
